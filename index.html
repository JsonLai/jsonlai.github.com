About Us

Future Innov

FICompany is a provider of reliable visual management services for the global IoT supply chain, with visual products at its core. The company independently designs, develops, produces, and sells complex intelligent code reading products, and is an explorer in the fields of QR code recognition and machine vision. Among them, the independently developed DPM series products have injected new energy into the booming field of DPM codes in China.

The intelligent code reading products under the company are widely used in industries such as 3C, logistics, automotive, LCD, new energy, medicine, etc., meeting the needs of users for production visualization management and information traceability.
